North Vancouver Tree Permit Requirements

The rules are completely different depending on whether your property is in the District of North Vancouver (DNV) or the City of North Vancouver (CNV). Select your municipality below.

🏔️

District of North Vancouver

Tree Protection Bylaw 7671

  • Permit required for trees 75cm+ diameter
  • Protected species always need a permit
  • Steep slopes and ravines trigger permits
  • $300 application fee
DNV Permit Guide →
🏙️

City of North Vancouver

Tree Bylaw No. 8888

  • Rules depend on your property zoning
  • Low density residential often exempt
  • Other zones: permit for trees 20cm+
  • Pruning restrictions apply to all
CNV Permit Guide →
Not sure which municipality you're in? The District and the City of North Vancouver share a border and are easy to confuse. Search your address on Google Maps and look for "District of North Vancouver" or "City of North Vancouver" in the result. You can also call DNV at 604-990-2311 or CNV at 604-985-7761 to confirm.

Key Differences at a Glance

Rule District (DNV) City (CNV)
Governing Bylaw Tree Protection Bylaw 7671 Tree Bylaw No. 8888
Size Threshold 75cm diameter 20cm diameter (non-residential)
Residential Exemption No blanket exemption RS/RT zones generally exempt
Application Fee $300 Varies by zone
Protected Species 6 species always protected Zoning-dependent
Contact 604-990-2311 TreeBylaw@cnv.org
