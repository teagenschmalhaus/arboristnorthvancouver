For Homeowners

Have a tree concern? The fastest way to get help is through our assessment tool. You'll receive guidance specific to your situation and connect with qualified arborists if needed.

For Arborists

Interested in joining our network? We partner with licensed, insured arborists serving North Vancouver and surrounding areas. Requirements include:

  • Valid BC business license
  • Minimum $2M liability insurance
  • WorkSafeBC coverage
  • ISA certification (preferred)

Use the contact form to inquire about partnership opportunities.

Important Note

We are not a tree service company. We don't perform tree work, provide emergency services, or dispatch crews. We're an educational resource and referral service.

If you have a tree emergency, please call 911 (life-threatening), BC Hydro at 1-888-769-3766 (power lines), or use our emergency guide to find 24/7 arborists.
